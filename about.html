about: danger, inc

Danger, Inc is in no way affiliated with that company that made sidekicks, got bought by Microsoft, and then was no more. It's a name from my childhood, when a friend and I were imagining what it would be like to run a company.

I have my own company now, but this isn't it. This is a place on the Internet to publish various technical and cultural thoughts.
